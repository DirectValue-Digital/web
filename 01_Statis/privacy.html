Privacy Policy for Statis: Minimal System Monitor

Effective Date: 2026-01-30

We are pleased that you are using Statis: Minimal System Monitor. Protecting your privacy is extremely important to us. This privacy policy explains what information the app processes.

1. No Collection of Personal Data

Statis: Minimal System Monitor does not collect, store, use, or share any personal data. We do not track your usage behavior, we do not use any analysis tools (such as Google Analytics or Firebase), and we have no access to your identity.

2. Access to System Data

The app accesses system information on your Mac purely locally to fulfill its core functions:

  • CPU & RAM Usage: To display current system performance.
  • Disk Storage (SSD): To display available storage space.
  • Battery Status: To display current charge level and health.
  • Temperatures: To display the thermal load of the system.

This data is processed in real-time on your device and is never transmitted to us or third parties. Once you close the app, these temporary readings are discarded.

3. No Internet Connection Required

The app functions completely offline. No data is sent to external servers.

4. No Cookies

This website and the app do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

As technologies and laws can change, we may update this policy occasionally. The latest version is always available at this URL.

Wir freuen uns, dass Sie Statis: Minimal System Monitor nutzen. Der Schutz Ihrer Privatsphäre ist uns extrem wichtig. In dieser Datenschutzerklärung erläutern wir, welche Informationen die App verarbeitet.

1. Keine Erfassung personenbezogener Daten

Statis: Minimal System Monitor sammelt, speichert, nutzt oder teilt keine personenbezogenen Daten. Wir tracken Ihr Nutzungsverhalten nicht, wir verwenden keine Analyse-Tools (wie Google Analytics oder Firebase) und wir haben keinen Zugriff auf Ihre Identität.

Die App greift ausschließlich lokal auf Systeminformationen Ihres Macs zu, um ihre Kernfunktionen zu erfüllen:

  • CPU & RAM-Auslastung: Um die aktuelle Systemleistung anzuzeigen.
